# Member User Guide

## Overview
The Member Dashboard provides Knights with access to their profile, event schedules, and donation history.

## Core Features

### 1. Digital Identification
**Navigation:** `Attendance > My QR Code`
- Your **Unique QR Code** is your digital ID.
- Present this code to Officers at events for automated attendance logging.

### 2. Events
**Navigation:** `Events`
- **Calendar**: View upcoming council and global events.
- **Register**: RSVP to events you plan to attend.
- **History**: View your past event attendance record.

### 3. Donations
**Navigation:** `Donations > Make a Donation`
- **GCash**: Donate securely online using GCash integration.
- **Manual**: Log a cash donation for Officer verification.
- **Receipts**: Download blockchain-verified receipts for your records.

### 4. Profile Management
**Navigation:** `Profile` (Top Right)
- **Edit Details**: Update your address, contact info, and profile picture.
- **Degree Status**: View your current degree (1st-4th).

### 5. Community
**Navigation:** `Forum`
- Participate in discussions.
- Vote on polls (if enabled).
- Stay updated with Council announcements.
