# Officer User Guide

## Overview
The Officer Dashboard is designed for Grand Knights and Council Officers to manage day-to-day operations of their specific council.

## Core Features

### 1. Event Management
**Navigation:** `Events`
- **Propose Event**: Click `New Event`. Fill in details (Name, Date, Location, Budget) and submit for Admin approval.
- **Manage Attendance**:
  - Go to `Attendance > Take Attendance`.
  - Use the **QR Code Scanner** to scan members' digital IDs at the event entrance.
  - Manually mark attendance if necessary.

### 2. Recruitment
**Navigation:** `Analytics > My Recruits` or `Dashboard`
- **Add Recruit**: Submit a new member application. You will be listed as their recruiter.
- **Track Progress**: View the status of your recruits (Pending -> Approved).

### 3. Member Oversight
**Navigation:** `Management > Council Members`
- View a list of all members in your council.
- Monitor member activity status (Active vs Inactive).
- **Update Degrees**: Promote members (e.g., 1st Degree -> 2nd Degree) after they complete ceremonies.

### 4. Donations
- **Manual Entry**: Record cash/check donations received offline via `Donations > Manual Entry`.
- **Review**: Validate manual donations submitted by members before they are added to the blockchain.

## Communication
- **Forums**: Post announcements in the Council Forum.
- **Notifications**: Receive alerts when your events are approved or when members join your council.
